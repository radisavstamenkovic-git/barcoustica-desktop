Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Radisav Stamenkovic (“we”, “our”, “the developer”) handles user data in the Barcoustica Android application and Barcoustica Desktop Player.

1. Data Collection

The app does not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

Specifically, the app does NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Photos, media, or files
  • Contacts
  • Advertising IDs
  • Analytics data

The app operates fully offline and only plays audio files provided by the user via the connected desktop application.

2. How the App Works

The application transfers music files only between the user’s Android device and the Barcoustica Desktop Player using a local connection. No data is sent to our servers or third-party services.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties.

4. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data, no personal information is stored on servers. All audio files remain on the user's own devices.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

The app retains no data on external servers. All files remain under the user’s control and can be deleted at any time directly from the user’s device.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not target children and does not collect personal information from users of any age.

For privacy-related questions or requests, users can contact us at:
